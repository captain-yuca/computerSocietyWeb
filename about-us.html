About Us

We are the IEEE Computer Society Chapter of the UPRM Branch.
Our Vision is to be the leading provider of technical information, community services, and personalized services to the world's computing professionals.
Our Goal is to be essential to the global technical community and computer professionals everywhere and be universally recognized for the contributions of technical professionals in developing and applying technology to improve global conditions.
We want to provide future professionals the tools they need to be successful in the computing field.

Steps for Joining Us

To Join Us just follow these steps!

  1. Firstly, you must sign up for the IEEE membership using one of the following methods:
    • Go to our offices to help you sign up.
    • Sign up online at http://www.ieee.org/go/join_student. Create a new account and then fill up the membership.
    • Write down your member number since you will need it to fill out the next step.

  2. Fill out this form to sign up for the UPRM Computer Society Branch and recieve our newsletter and announcements we might do in the future.

  3. Everything is set! For any questions please contact us!
